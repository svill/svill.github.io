Bookings made simple

Allow your customers to book an appointment with you.

Save the hassle with automated bookings

Communicating back and forth with customers can be a pain, with Rizerve you can easily let customers choose a time that suits you both.

Customizable time slots

Allocate time slots according to your needs. Allow customers to select from slots from as small as 15 minutes to a full day.

Block-out breaks

Give yourself the time you deserve. Block-out customers from selecting time slots during your lunch break or days away.

Show a selection of services

Allow customers to choose from a selection of services you offer. Catering for all industries, Rizerve allows you to define your services in any way you like. Alternatively, choose and customize pre-set services for Hair, Nails, Lashes, Waxing, Massage, Wellness, Mechanics and much more.

Manage all your appointments seamlessly

View, approve and reject bookings all from a simple user interface. See your calendar and plan accordingly.

Notifications

Instantly receive email or SMS notifications when appointments are made. Alternatively, configure daily summaries to be emailed instead.

Personalizable page link

Customize a URL that expresses your business. Show and share a link for customers to visit and make appointments.

Built with security in mind

Every appointment and contact added to Rizerve is securely encrypted and stored using the latest security practices.

Rizerve uses secure cloud-based networks meaning you can safely access your appointments from anywhere.

Eliminate the need to manually create backups knowing your data is secured remotely.

All data is stored in compliance-certified data centers adhering to globally recognised security standards including ISO 27001.
